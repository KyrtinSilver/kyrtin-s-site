As the “investors” who bought into and/or doubled-down on those overt frauds are fully responsible for their own actions, foolish as they may be, any controlled demolition includes the implosion of their financial interests, not their protection.

Rather, it is the general public, all those who were given no choice and held no responsibility over these matters, who must be protected from the fallout of such controlled implosions whenever possible. In cases like SoftBank, this may only be possible to limited degrees, but entities like SoftBank are themselves highly visible in their own deranged behaviors, with many pointing to their leader holding a “messiah complex”.
