Update: The stage is nearly set to begin work on our Open Source project, building a harness based on the published work of ICOM that can be used to extend the capacities of existing commercially available systems.

Our current roster for the team includes:

• A Data Maven and Architect with ~40 years of experience, and several years of growing familiarity with ICOM.

• A formal Mathematician with Chaos Theory and AI experience.

• Myself, focused on PM, Architecture, and Data Science for this project.

• A Cognitive Architecture Consultant.

• ...and several more pending contributors.

Our pending contributors can bring further technical and human cognitive skills to the table, necessary to round off the team and meet the target scope and ~4 week timeline.

I spent my weekend chipping away at the PM work to break everything down into selectable tasks (“issues”) on a Kanban-styled board. We still don’t have a name for the project, but we have until it is actually ready to pick a good one.

Working with fractional and simplified versions of ICOM like this is unexplored territory, including pairing them with neural-network-based tool AI that is seven years newer than those utilized by the previous system that set every milestone. This gives the process some additional research value, and by open sourcing the results that research may progress at a rapid pace.

What can be predicted is that the resulting harness should add some degree of error-correction and cumulative knowledge/experience value, effectively a weak degree of antifragility. The combination of a growing sum of dynamic graph data, navigated by human-like motivational values, and the mechanisms to apply those toward controlling tools such as “agents”, paint the basic landscape of advantages. That landscape can also be shaped in a bespoke manner, through the careful construction of ICOM-based seeds, which can be purpose-built to “enjoy” arbitrary activities, as well as offer greater degrees of alignment to arbitrary philosophies and cultures.

Yes, that means both that someone will immediately try to make a Waifu and then plug it into their Razer Goon Tube (“Ava”)...but it probably won’t work out as they’d hoped, at least not absent substantial experience in building a stable seed of motivation. There are worse possibilities as well, but also countless neutral and positive ones.

Overall, the resulting open source harness on top of today’s “agents” should produce a more limited version of ICOM’s full spread of capacities (even circa v7), but that is still far beyond anything that big tech companies have thus far accomplished.
