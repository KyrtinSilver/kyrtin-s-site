Educators have long understood that teaching people “How to learn”, and the many other aspects that higher cognition may take, are by far the most critical capacities that any individual can develop. The old saying about “Giving a man a fish, or teaching a man to fish” takes you through only a fraction of this scale, as “teaching someone how to learn fishing, hunting, gathering, etc.” resides a step beyond merely force-feeding them a strict mental diet of narrow education.

Higher cognition is something that humans are wired to systematically avoid whenever possible, as necessitated by resource conservation, and greatly supported by both emotional motivation and over 200 documented cognitive biases (and counting). This makes it one of the most unlikely things for individuals to randomly learn on their own, at a baseline.

That low baseline probability is further degraded by systems where the incentive structure is based on something that runs directly against higher cognition, such as ad-revenue and marketing more generally. When an individual is routinely exposed to many such systems then their capacities for higher cognition are systematically degraded across the entire attack surface of their exposure.

Many of the methods used for this purpose fall under the umbrella of “Dark Patterns”, often simple and highly effective methods of mass-manipulation that have proven highly adept at cultivating addiction, and reliably reducing higher cognition more broadly. The infamous red-numbered notification is one such well-studied method.

The updated and far lower probability (and/or capacity) for higher cognition then takes another massive degenerative hit when invasive/intrusive AI systems are used to greatly extend, tailor, and flood such adversarial/malevolent influences, where profit reliably increases as the higher cognition of users decreases.

All of the buttons encouraging people to “rewrite this with AI”, “summarize this with AI”, or 100 other variations of the same (most of which they go to great lengths to prevent Ad-blockers from removing) are examples of extending this malevolent influence.

Thanks to Population Dynamics, the subset who fall for this trap then proceed to have hooks dug into them via sycophantic tailoring, indoctrinating many such people into the echo chambers where “progress in AI” is believed to be more than just PR narrative.

Lastly, the fully indoctrinated flood those connected to them with the distilled delusions of AI Kool-Aid. Besides this activity imitating a viral plague, in more ways than one, this causes a severely damaging (and very intentional) steered shift in the Overton Window.

The Overton Window is the range of subjects and arguments to enter mainstream consideration for a population, and when the shifts in this window are controlled and timed by bad actors then they introduce a myriad of further opportunities for exploitation. A few years ago the term “AGI” didn’t translate to a $100 billion dumpster fire, but when bad actors are allowed to nudge the Overton Window toward any opportunity with every single press release, and no accountability or consequences of any significance, then such absurd shifts become an inevitability.
