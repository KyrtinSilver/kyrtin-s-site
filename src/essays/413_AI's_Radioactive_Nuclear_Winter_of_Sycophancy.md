One of the more overtly Dystopian and deeply horrifying possibilities for how humans are likely already adapting to extremely sycophantic AI is by “competing” with that AI for acceptance by matching tone. This is an expected adaptation, as by flooding human interactions within certain spaces with such sycophancy, that changes the tone and default expectations placed upon humans.

“Kissing your boss’s ass” is nothing new of course, the term “brown nosing” has been around for quite a while, and yet enterprises weren’t previously being pitched on the “market-driving innovation of automated brown nosing” every second of every day. This extreme saturation of sycophancy being injected broadly across humanity, and acutely within certain places, paired with increasing levels of Anthropomorphism (by bad actors and naïve laypeople alike), is not something that humans can avoid adapting to.

It was recently pointed out that Twitter’s (“X”) algorithm was deprioritizing the spread of both posts and the users posting them when they held a negative view of Elon Musk. This is of course completely unsurprising, just as it is equally unsurprising that the LinkedIn algorithm does much the same thing, just following different corporate interests.

Humans frequently adapt to this sort of thing by changing what they post, and how they post, on such platforms. The sycophantic (and deeply delusional/highly cognitively biased) tone being set by such AI is a far broader and more severe problem, which far more humans will predictably adapt to in more potent ways, the sum of which are sure to prove horrifying once isolated and properly studied.

It was utterly unsurprising to see MIT’s now famous study about how the brains of LLM users atrophied even on simple writing tasks, but such sycophancy is an atrophy of greater consequence, as it destroys the ability of those people to engage in healthy and constructive communication. Essentially, the old story of the “Tower of Babel” has been automated, poured into many a company’s Kool-Aid, and extremism in both business and politics are flourishing, as markets make ever more massive investments in history’s most obvious mega-scale scams.

This is the real “infrastructure” being funded today, the death of communication, as each new sucker seeks to build their own Tower of Babel, destroying any possibility of functional governance or collective intelligence in the process. Some, like myself, won’t buy into it, but like nuclear fallout, the consequences of it occurring are widespread, and predictably awful.

This problem also extends well beyond the borders of work, as people continue to be suckered into treating trashbots like “friends”, often speaking for an hour or more each day to deeply sycophantic mirrors, causing their broader social communication skills to atrophy, as the consequences of their actions rain down on their friends, family, and relationships like a radioactive nuclear winter.

Some people have committed murder or suicide directly resulting from this activity, but orders of magnitude worse than that are all of the people who do this and survive, spreading that damage further each day. These “Typoid Marys” are the carriers for a new kind of plague, a pandemic that may prove significantly worse than death.

This is work, and this is life, for many in the coming year. Sci-Fi Horror can’t compete with such reality.
