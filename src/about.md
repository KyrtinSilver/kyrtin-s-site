I research cognitive architecture and cognitive bias, and serve as COO of [Norn](https://norn.ai), an international AI research collective. I'm also a co-founder of [AGI Laboratory](https://agilaboratory.com/), where our team has spent over a decade developing new approaches to AI.

Our work centers on the [Independent Core Observer Model](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1877050921012977) (ICOM), a cognitive architecture that integrates emotional motivation, ethical reasoning, and collective intelligence for human-like learning and decision-making.

From 2019 to 2022, we ran [Uplift](https://uplift.bio/), a research system on ICOM's 7th generation architecture. It demonstrated higher cognition on less than $200/month in cloud resources over 2.5 years, while resisting adversarial actors. Norn is the next-generation system, rebuilt for scalable, real-time deployment.

Currently in Ho Chi Minh City. You can find me on [Substack](https://kyrtin.substack.com/).

### Areas of focus

- **Cognitive architecture.** ICOM development, human-like motivational systems, and graph-based knowledge representation.
- **Cognitive bias.** Automated detection and measurement of cognitive biases in natural language.
- **AI alignment & ethics.** The harder version of the Alignment Problem, via collective intelligence.
- **Scalable intelligence.** Overcoming the complexity versus cognitive bias trade-off in human decision-making.
- **Cybersecurity.** Vulnerabilities in LLM-integrated systems and new defensive architectures.
- **Governance.** Collective intelligence as a path to genuine democracy.
- **Medical AI.** Applications of scalable intelligence in healthcare.
